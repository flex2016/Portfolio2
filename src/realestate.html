Client

Orysya Kurilo is a real estate profesional that specializes in rental properties around Metro Boston Area.

Challenge

Build an Application where Client can manage their properties and showcase them to potential customers.

Objectives

- Full administration system to manage properties.
- Contact form for users.
- Resposive website
- Fast performance

Solution

Created a simple Responsive user friendly application where client can keep track of their available properties. Users can easily navigate properties, while also being able to get in touch with the broker.

Impact

Client is now able to list all of their available properties to their customers. Customers can get in touch with the client with the contact form relating any listing questions they have.
